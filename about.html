    Because We Care About You!

    Patients first: We put the patient first in all we do. No matter where in the hospital we work, we remember always that caring for patients and their families is at the heart of our mission and our philosophy. Integrity: We adhere to an uncompromising code of ethics that emphasizes honesty, transparency and sincerity. Through our words and actions, we earn the trust of our patients and their families, our community and our coworkers. We seek to do the right thing, always and everywhere, in our day-to-day work and lives. Excellence: We continuously strive for excellence. We never stop learning and working to improve our skills, programs and services. Teamwork: We can achieve our mission and goals only by working together. Through the collective and coordinated efforts of our staff, we apply our diverse talents, backgrounds, ideas and experiences to create solutions and benefit patients. We value team success over individual success.

    MediWay is dedicated to providing the most advanced healthcare to the communities and patients we serve. We are bonded in an essential academic and service relationship with our hospitals. We are a diverse group of people, joined together by a shared set of values. We put the patients first in all we do.
